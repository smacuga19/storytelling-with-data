⚡ How to use
Quick Start
1
Drag a component from the left panel onto the canvas.
2
Press W (or click ✎ Wire) to enter wire mode. Click a terminal or wire endpoint to start, click another to finish. Press S to return to Select.
3
Add a Ground component and wire it in. Then press ▶ Run (or Enter) to simulate — voltages appear in green, currents in amber.
4
Double-click any component to edit its value.
5
Press P to enter Probe mode. Click two nodes to measure voltage between them.
Every circuit needs at least one Ground node and a closed DC path or the solver will warn you.
🖱 Pan: Space + drag, or middle-mouse drag. Zoom: scroll wheel.
